How GoCredi Works

The model and methodology behind your credit evaluation

How the Model Works

GoCredi uses a Random Forest classifier trained on real credit card application data. It evaluates your personal and financial profile to estimate the likelihood of approval.

The training pipeline includes:

  • StandardScaler — normalizes numerical inputs to a consistent scale
  • OneHotEncoder — converts categorical values into numerical features
  • SMOTE — balances the training data to reduce class bias
  • RandomizedSearchCV — tunes hyperparameters for optimal model performance

Input Features

The model relies on 10 features chosen for strong predictive value:

Gender Car Ownership Property Ownership Education Level Income Type Family Status Annual Income Age Years Employed Family Members

What You Get

Each result includes four outputs to help you understand your evaluation:

  • Approval Decision — Approved or Not Approved
  • Risk Level — Low, Medium, or High risk classification
  • Probability Breakdown — approval and rejection percentages
  • Top Model Factors — the three features most influential to the prediction
