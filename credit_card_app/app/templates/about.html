About GoCredi

Understanding the model behind your credit evaluation

How the Model Works

GoCredi uses a Random Forest classifier trained on real credit card application data. The model evaluates your personal and financial profile to estimate the likelihood of credit card approval.

The training pipeline includes:

  • StandardScaler — normalizes numerical inputs
  • OneHotEncoder — encodes categorical features
  • SMOTE — balances the training dataset
  • RandomizedSearchCV — used during experimentation for model evaluation and tuning

Input Features

The model uses 10 features selected for predictive performance:

Gender Car Ownership Property Ownership Education Level Income Type Family Status Annual Income Age Years Employed Family Members

What You Get

After submitting your information, GoCredi provides:

  • Approved / Not Approved — model prediction
  • Risk Level — Low, Medium, or High
  • Probability breakdown — acceptance vs rejection
  • Top model factors — the most influential features globally (not per-user explanation)
